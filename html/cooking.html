Pizza

  1. Take one pita
  2. Open up the pizza
  3. Take one pizza sauce and put it on the pita
  4. Take one or two slices of cheese and rip them up
  5. Put the pieces on the sauce
  6. Put the pita in the oven for 15 minutes
  7. Enjoy!
